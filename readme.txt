Unique UX allows wordpress sites to present unique, personalised content for each visitor.

== Description ==

Fact - Content tailored to a users specific requirements generates higher conversion rates.
But how do you achieve that when the content inside a wordpress page or post is the same for each user?
With Unique UX, thats how!

1. Personalise content based on location
1. Personalise content based on browsing history
1. Personalise content based on device
1. Personalise content based on schedules
1. And much more to come...

== Installation ==


1. Unzip uniqueux.zip and upload the folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Visit the "UniqueUX" page on the main menu
1. Add at least one visitor group
1. Add at least one content group
1. Display your unique content through shortcodes or widgets

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Who should install our plugin =

Anyone who is seeking to improve their UX by presenting unique content to each user.
